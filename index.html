What is the NOPAT?
The NOPAT calculator is a tool that determines the operating efficiency of a company.
How to calculate NOPAT?
To calculate NOPAT you should use the following formula:
NOPAT = operating income * (1 - tax rate)
Why use NOPAT?
The NOPAT calculator is used in order to measure the operating efficiency of a company.
